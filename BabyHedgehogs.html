Hedgehog Headquarters: Baby Hedgehogs!

Baby hedgehogs are very tiny, and very adorable! So here's some facts about them, and a small selection of endearing photos!

Baby Hedgehog Facts:

  1. They are born pink
  2. Baby hedgehogs are called hoglets!
  3. They have about 5,000 spikes
  4. A group of hedgehogs is called an "array"!
